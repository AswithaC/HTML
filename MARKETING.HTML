Marketing

Marketing is one of the oldest professions. Marketing is relatively new although it is an old subject and has emerged from the older concept of exchange.
The complex marketing system of to-day has evolved through different stages of economic development.
To-day, marketing is a complex activity because a marketer has to coordinate and manage several things before reaching the target market.

Customers

A lot of changes occur in consumer habits, tastes and aspirations and these changes are visible everywhere.
Consumers in developing countries like India tend to emulate high material standards of living from advanced western societies.
Automobiles, consumer electronics, household appliances, computers, cell phones etc., have become common, consumer products of daily use.
